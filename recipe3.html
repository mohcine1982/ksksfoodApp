grilled chicken

Ingredients
  • A large chicken.
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • half cup of olive oil.
  • Teaspoon grated ginger.
  • Half a spoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of crushed garlic.
  • Saffron.
  •  salt
  •  coriander chopped 
  •  Parsley, chopped.
  • A teaspoon of ghee
  • Lemon juice.
method
  • We wash the chicken with the liver and heart with water well,
  • Then we rub the chicken inside and outside with salt,
  • Then we put water in a deep bowl and add half a cup of vinegar to it,
  • And lemon juice with lemon peel and a spoonful of flour
  • We put the chicken in the bowl and let it steep for an hour,
  • Then we lift the chicken and the offal from the solution and wash it with water,
  • Drain the chicken well from the water.
  • We put in oil, ginger, black pepper, crushed garlic,
  • Saffron, tincture, and Moroccan ghee,
  • Add the coriander and parsley in a saucepan over low heat and heat them slightly.
  • Then remove the pan from the heat and add the salt and lemon juice and stir the ingredients.
  • We put the marinade on the chicken and spread it well on the outer and inner sides of the chicken.
  • Put the chopped onions in a regular pressure cooker or saucepan
  • Add a little ginger, salt, and pepper, and if something remains of the marinade, add it
  • We put the chicken and liver over the onions and add a small cup of water.
  • Put the pot over the medium heat, stirring the chicken
  • Every now and then until you start reddening, and put a little water (about a cup),
  • And let the chicken cook for forty-five minutes if it is a pressure cooker,
  • If it was a normal pot, the chicken would need fifty minutes to an hour.
  • The sauce should be thick. If the broth is large, we remove the chicken from the pot
  • Leave the broth on high heat until thick.
  • We put the chicken in a serving dish and pour the sauce over it
  • Decorate the chicken with pickled lemon and olives.

Moroccan tagine with chicken and vegetables

Ingredients
  • A kilo of chicken cut into four pieces
  •  garlic
  • 2 tomato
  • 2 potatos
  • 1/4kg carrots
  • Parsley, chopped
  • Coriander, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • Teaspoon for each of:
  • (Garlic powder + red pepper + cumin + ginger + turmeric)
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of ordinary oil
method
  • Cut onions and tomatoes slices or pieces, and sprinkle them with salt and pepper
  • Cut the remaining vegetables and soak them in a mixture of
  • (Tablespoon salt + spoon of black pepper + spoon of garlic powder + spoon of red pepper and spoon of parsley coriander).
  • We put the chicken in a saucepan. Grease chicken and olive oil with grated garlic and a tablespoon of salt + black pepper spoon + ginger spoon + turmeric spoon + cumin spoon + coriander and parsley.
  • Let them cook over medium heat until the chicken turns red
  • Remove the mixture from the bowl, then put the onions, then the carrots, then the chicken.
  • Then add tomatoes, then potatoes, then put a little hot water, then close under low heat for about 80 minutes.
  • finally. We offer chicken tagine with vegetables

Ground Beef Couscous

A quick, one-pot meal, this ground beef couscous is perfect for busy weeknights. It's full of flavor, protein, and nutritious vegetables, making it a family favorite.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 2 cups pearl couscous
  • 12 oz lean ground beef
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken or beef stock
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Seasonings & Herbs

  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger (optional)
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil or parsley, chopped

Preparation Method

1. Brown the Ground Beef

  1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add ground beef and cook for 5-7 minutes, breaking it apart until browned.
  3. Drain any excess fat, if needed.

2. Sauté the Vegetables

  1. Add onions, bell peppers, and garlic to the skillet. Sauté for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  2. Stir in paprika, thyme, garlic powder, turmeric, and ginger. Cook for 1 more minute to enhance the flavors.

3. Cook the Couscous

  1. Pour in diced tomatoes and stock. Bring to a boil.
  2. Stir in pearl couscous, cover, and reduce heat to low.
  3. Simmer for 10-12 minutes, or until the liquid is absorbed and couscous is tender.

4. Final Touches & Serving

  1. Fluff the couscous with a fork. Stir in fresh basil or parsley.
  2. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  3. Serve hot, garnished with extra herbs.

Serving Suggestions

  • Pair with a simple green salad or yogurt-based sauce like tzatziki.
  • Enjoy with warm pita bread for a complete meal.

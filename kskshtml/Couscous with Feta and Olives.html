Greek-Style Couscous with Feta and Olives

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked couscous
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp chopped fresh oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the Couscous: Begin by cooking 1 cup of couscous according to package instructions. Typically, this involves bringing 1 cup of water or vegetable broth to a boil, then stirring in the couscous, covering it, and letting it steam for 5-10 minutes until fluffy.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables and Cheese: While the couscous is cooking, halve the cherry tomatoes, pit and halve the Kalamata olives, and crumble the feta cheese.

  3. Mix the Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the cooked couscous, feta cheese, cherry tomatoes, and olives.

  4. Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, olive oil, chopped oregano, salt, and pepper.

  5. Toss and Serve: Pour the dressing over the couscous mixture and toss everything together until well combined.

  6. Taste and Adjust: Taste your salad and adjust seasoning if needed, adding more salt, pepper, or lemon juice to suit your taste.

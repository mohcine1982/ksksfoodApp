Palestinian Couscous Chicken Legs Recipe

This Palestinian couscous chicken legs recipe brings together tender chicken, fluffy couscous, and rich Middle Eastern spices for a comforting, flavorful dish. It's a true taste of Palestinian culinary tradition and a must-try for lovers of Mediterranean food.

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken legs
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp olive oil (for searing)

For the Couscous

  • 1 ½ cups pearl couscous (or Israeli couscous)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (to fluff couscous)

For the Vegetables

  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

For the Cooking Liquid

  • 2 cups chicken broth (or vegetable broth for a vegetarian version)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

1. Prepare the Chicken

  1. Rinse the chicken legs under cool water and pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, cumin, coriander, and cinnamon.
  3. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the chicken legs and sear for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown. Remove from the pan and set aside.

2. Prepare the Couscous

  1. While searing the chicken, bring a pot of 2 cups salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the couscous and stir. Once it starts boiling, cover the pot and remove from heat.
  3. Let the couscous sit for 5-7 minutes, allowing it to absorb the liquid.
  4. Fluff the couscous with a fork and drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil to prevent sticking. Set aside.

3. Sauté the Vegetables

  1. In the same skillet, add 1 diced onion, 1 diced bell pepper, and 2 minced garlic cloves.
  2. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until fragrant and softened.
  3. Add 2 diced tomatoes and 1 can chickpeas. Stir to combine.

4. Simmer the Chicken

  1. Pour in 2 cups chicken broth and add 1 tbsp lemon juice. Bring to a simmer.
  2. Return the chicken legs to the skillet. Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked through (internal temperature should reach 165°F/74°C).

5. Final Assembly

  1. On a large serving platter or individual plates, arrange the couscous.
  2. Place the cooked chicken legs on top of the couscous.
  3. Garnish with fresh parsley and drizzle the cooking liquid over the dish.

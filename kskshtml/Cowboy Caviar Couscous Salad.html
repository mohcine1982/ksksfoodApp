Cowboy Caviar Couscous Salad Recipe

A refreshing and colorful salad combining the bold flavors of Tex-Mex with the light, fluffy texture of Mediterranean couscous. Perfect for summer gatherings, meal prep, or as a side dish.

Ingredients

Salad Base

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 orange bell peppers, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen)
  • 1 cup black-eyed peas, drained and rinsed

Dressing

  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/3 cup lime juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 3 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce (optional, adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the Couscous

    • Bring 1 cup of water to a boil.
    • Add the couscous, cover, and remove from heat. Let it sit for 5 minutes until the liquid is absorbed. Fluff with a fork.

  2. Prepare the Vegetables

    • While the couscous is resting, chop the red onion, bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and avocado into small pieces.

  3. Make the Dressing

    • In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, cilantro, hot sauce, cumin, and salt until well combined.

  4. Combine Everything

    • In a large bowl, mix the cooked couscous, black beans, corn, black-eyed peas, bell peppers, tomatoes, and avocado.
    • Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all ingredients evenly.

  5. Chill & Serve

    • For the best flavor, refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
    • Serve as a light meal, side dish, or potluck favorite.

Storage Tips

  • Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
  • Add avocado just before serving to prevent browning.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with grilled meats, veggie burgers, or tortilla chips.
  • Enjoy as a healthy lunch or refreshing summer meal.

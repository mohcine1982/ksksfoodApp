Chicken tagine with dried plum and almonds

Ingredients
  • Medium washed and clean chicken
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • Dried plum
  • Teaspoon cinnamon
  • Two onions, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of turmeric and turmeric
  • A cup of peeled and chopped almonds
  • Vegetable oil for frying
method
  • Cut the chicken's wings and thighs, leaving the chicken breast in one piece.
  • Grease the pieces with salt, pepper and cumin and leave for an hour.
  • In a small skillet, put the dried plum, then add cold water until the plum is covered
  • Add cinnamon, reduce heat and leave the mixture for 10 minutes.
  • Place the onions in a wide skillet with turmeric, ginger, salt and pepper with ¼ cup of water.
  • Cover and cook for 15 minutes.
  • Then fry almonds in 4-5 tablespoons of oil.
  • Extract it and iron the chicken in the same oil and then the onions.
  • Cover the meal with cooking paper and cook over a very low heat for 1-1 / ¼ hour.
  • After removing the paper, add the cooked, dried prunes to the pan and cook a little.
  • Arrange the chicken breasts in the center of the serving dish,
  • Place chicken thighs and wings around it and cover with plums and sauce.
  • Spread almonds and serve.
